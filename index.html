Silver Mode Concept

Driving independence for the silver generation.

Silver Mode reimagines assisted driving for older adults who still love to drive, but feel overwhelmed by touchscreen-heavy vehicle interfaces.

The Problem

Modern vehicle software can feel intimidating when confidence matters most.

Too many choices at once

Deep menus and crowded screens can turn simple tasks into stressful moments.

Assistance is powerful, UI is not

Assisted driving can reduce fatigue, but the interface still expects fast, precise interaction.

Stress scales on long drives

Routes with unfamiliar roads, weather shifts, and charging stops increase cognitive load.

A Personal Reason

“My grandpa can still drive, but long drives like Provo to St. George can feel stressful. Assisted driving could be life changing for people like him, but only if the technology itself feels simple, calm, and approachable.”

Silver Mode began as a simple question: what if assisted driving felt less like software, and more like a trusted co-pilot?

The Solution

A simplified interface centered on the essentials.

Essential actions only

  • Drive
  • Reverse
  • Park
  • Music
  • Destination
  • Battery
  • Call family / help

Designed for confidence

Larger touch targets, stronger contrast, voice-first prompts, and calmer visual hierarchy reduce pressure behind the wheel.
